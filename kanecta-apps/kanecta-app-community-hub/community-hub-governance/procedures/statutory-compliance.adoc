= Statutory Compliance
:doctype: article

This procedure ensures the Society meets its legal obligations as an incorporated society under the Incorporated Societies Act 2022.

== Annual return

The Society must file an annual return with the Registrar of Incorporated Societies within six months of the end of each financial year. The Secretary is responsible for this.

The annual return includes:

* Confirmation that the Society has held an AGM
* Current contact persons
* Current registered address
* Confirmation that the Society is still operating
* Any changes to the constitution during the year

The Secretary sets a calendar reminder 8 weeks before the due date and files at least 2 weeks early.

== Register of members

The Society must maintain a register of members as required by the 2022 Act. The register includes:

* Full name of each member
* Date of joining
* Membership status (Voting Member or otherwise)
* Date of resignation or removal (if applicable)

The register is maintained by the Secretary, kept secure, and not published publicly. Members may request to see their own entry.

== Constitution changes

Any amendment to the constitution must be:

. Passed by the required majority at a General Meeting (per the constitution's amendment clause)
. Filed with the Registrar within the timeframe required by the 2022 Act
. Published to all members promptly

The Secretary is responsible for filing constitutional amendments.

== Contact persons

The Society must maintain 1–3 contact persons registered with the Registrar. The Secretary ensures contact person details are current and updates the register promptly when they change.

== Winding up

If the Society resolves to wind up, it must follow the procedure in the Incorporated Societies Act 2022. Any remaining assets after debts are settled must be distributed as specified in the constitution — to another community organisation with similar purposes, not to members.

== Compliance calendar

The Secretary maintains a calendar of all statutory deadlines including:

* AGM deadline
* Annual return deadline
* Financial year end
* Any other reporting obligations

This calendar is shared with the Custodian Board and reviewed at the first quarterly meeting of each year.
