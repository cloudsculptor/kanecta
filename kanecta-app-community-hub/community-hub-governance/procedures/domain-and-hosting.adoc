= Domain and Hosting Management
:doctype: article

This procedure covers the ongoing management of featherston.co.nz and all hosting infrastructure. Continuity of the domain is central to the Society's purpose.

== Domain registration

* The domain featherston.co.nz must be registered to the Society, not to any individual.
* Renewal reminders must be set at least 60 days before expiry.
* The domain must be renewed promptly. Lapsing the domain is a critical failure — domain squatters monitor for expired domains constantly.
* Two volunteers must have independent access to the registrar account.

== DNS management

* All DNS changes must be documented before being applied.
* Changes should be made during low-traffic periods where possible.
* The current DNS configuration must be kept in the shared documentation.

== Hosting and infrastructure

* Hosting accounts must be held in the Society's name, not an individual's.
* At least two volunteers must have administrator access to all hosting accounts.
* Hosting renewal dates must be tracked in the shared infrastructure register.
* The administrator reviews hosting costs and contracts annually and reports to the Custodian Board.

== Credential management

* All credentials (domain registrar, hosting, DNS, server access) must be stored in the shared password manager.
* No critical credential may be held only by one person.
* When a volunteer steps back, their access is revoked and the shared credentials are rotated.

== Annual review

The administrator conducts an annual review of all infrastructure covering:

. Domain registration status and renewal dates
. Hosting contracts — costs, terms, and whether they remain appropriate
. All service accounts — which are active, which are no longer needed
. SSL certificate validity
. Access audit — confirming who has access to what, and revoking any that is no longer needed

The review is documented and shared with volunteers and the Custodian Board.
