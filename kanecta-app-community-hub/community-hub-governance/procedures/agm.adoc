= Annual General Meeting (AGM)
:doctype: article

This procedure covers how the AGM is planned, run, and recorded. The AGM is the Society's primary democratic event and must be conducted properly.

== Legal requirements

Under the Incorporated Societies Act 2022, the Society must hold an AGM within six months of the end of each financial year. The AGM must:

* Receive the annual financial statements
* Receive the Custodian Board's report on the year
* Conduct sortition to select the incoming Custodian Board
* Appoint officers for the coming year (Secretary, Treasurer, Contact Persons)
* Transact any other business of which proper notice has been given

== Timeline

[cols="1,3"]
|===
| 8 weeks before | Set the date and book a venue (physical or online)
| 6 weeks before | Publish notice of the AGM to all Voting Members
| 4 weeks before | Publish the agenda, financial statements, and Board report
| 30 days before | Open nominations for Custodian Board sortition pool
| 7 days before | Close nominations; confirm sortition pool
| AGM day | Run the meeting per the agenda below
| 14 days after | Publish minutes to all members
|===

== Running the meeting

. Open the meeting and confirm quorum (at least the number of Voting Members required by the constitution for a valid AGM).
. Confirm the agenda.
. Receive and note the financial statements.
. Receive and note the Board's annual report.
. Conduct sortition — draw five names from the confirmed pool of nominees.
. Appoint officers for the coming year.
. Transact any other notified business.
. Close the meeting.

== Sortition procedure

. The nominations list is read aloud and confirmed.
. Names are written on identical slips of paper (or a verified digital equivalent) and mixed thoroughly.
. A member who is not a nominee draws five names.
. Each drawn name is read aloud and confirmed as willing and available to serve.
. If a drawn person is unavailable, draw again until five confirmed members are selected.

== Minutes

Minutes must record: who attended, the financial statements received, the results of sortition (names of incoming Board), officers appointed, and any resolutions passed.

Minutes are published to all members within 14 days and retained permanently.
