# Constitution Drafting Notes

## Background

This constitution was drafted based on:
- The full chat history with Claude
- Other NZ Incorporated Societies constitutions
- NZ Companies Office templates
- Lessons learned and best practices from around the world

Output format: AsciiDoc, structured for NZ Companies Office submission.

---

## Format Approach

Every section follows this structure:
- **Brief** — the legal clause itself
- **TLDR / Design Rationale** — a callout box explaining the thinking behind it

This allows the founding members to make informed decisions about what to keep, change, or cut.

---

## Key Features Built Into the Draft

- **Anti-capture financial cap** — $20 max, no advertising; requires 75% supermajority to change
- **Sortition for the Custodian Board** — opt-in pool model, fully explained and legally defensible
- **Elected Site Coordinator** — two-term limit, instant-runoff voting
- **Recall mechanism** — 20% of Voting Members can trigger a removal vote
- **Open source + open data** — formal constitutional purposes, not just aspirations
- **Volunteer autonomy** — explicitly protected
- **Bootstrap clause** — honest about the self-appointed founding phase; 50-member trigger for formal democracy
